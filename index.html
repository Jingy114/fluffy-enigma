SEVENTEEN The Group

Seventeen is a kpop group consisting of 13 members: Mingyu, Joshua, Hoshi, Jeonghan, Woozi, Wonwoo, The8, S.Coups, Dino, Jun, Vernon, and DK, that debuted on May 26, 2015 under Pledis Entertainment.

    • Vocal Subunit

    The Vocal Subunit of Seventeen consists of members Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, Dk, and Seungkwan. As the name suggests, this subunit consists of the teams main and lead vocals. They mostly sing ballads for albums and concert performances. Each member in the vocal unit have very unique voices and styles of singing. Together, they make music that are able to help people get through dark times. Woozi, the leader of the vocal team, also takes the role of the producer for the group and currently has about 60 songs copyrighted under his name.

    Hip Hop Subunit

    The Hip Hop Subunit of Seventeen consists of members S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon. This group consists most of Seventeens rappers and their music genre-you guessed it, hip hop! Led by S.Coups who is also the leader of Seventeen, the hip-hop team is very cool and a lot of their lyrics are very powerful and meaningful. Through their raps, songs, and covers, they are able to display their hardships of debuting and moving up from rock bottom, as well as thanking their fans (Carats) for staying by their side.

    Performance Subunit

    The performance subunit of Seventeen consist of members Hoshi, The8, Jun, and Dino. The subunit is known for their amazing stage presence and charisma. Many of Seventeens choreographies are choreographed by Hoshi and the rest of the performance team. Seventeen is well known in the industry for the neatness of their choreographies, despite having so many members. This is due to their hard work and determination to present their best selves to their fans.
