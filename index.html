SEVENTEEN The Group

Seventeen is a kpop group consisting of 13 members: Mingyu, Joshua, Hoshi, Jeonghan, Woozi, Wonwoo, The8, S.Coups, Dino, Jun, Vernon, and DK, that debuted on May 26, 2015 under Pledis Entertainment.

    • Vocal Subunit

    The Vocal Subunit of Seventeen consists of members Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, Dk, and Seungkwan. As the name suggests, this subunit consists of the teams main and lead vocals. They mostly sing ballads for albums and concert performances. Each member in the vocal unit have very unique voices and styles of singing. Together, they make music that are able to help people get through dark times. Woozi, the leader of the vocal team, also takes the role of the producer for the group and currently has about 60 songs copyrighted under his name. Jeonghan, the second oldest of the group, takes the role of the lead vocalist and is known for his nickname "angel." Joshua (Hong Jisoo) was born in Los Angeles, California and went to Korea in 2013 to become a trainee. He is the groups lead vocalist and visual. DK (Lee Seokmin) is Seventeen's main vocalist and lead dancer and funniest member in the group (according to the other members). Lastly, Seungkwan is the main vocalist and youngest member of the vocal unit. He is considered the "mood maker" of the group.

    Hip Hop Subunit

    The Hip Hop Subunit of Seventeen consists of members S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon. This group consists most of Seventeens rappers and their music genre-you guessed it, hip hop! Led by S.Coups who is also the leader of Seventeen, the hip-hop team is very cool and a lot of their lyrics are very powerful and meaningful. Wonwoo is Seventeen's main vocalist, lead rapper, and lead dancer. Despite his cold appearance, his voice and personality is very soft. Mingyu is Seventeen's lead rapper, visual and the tallest member of the unit and of the entire group (187cm or 6'2"). He was also ranked number 27 on 'The 100 Most Handsome Faces of 2020.' The final member of the hip-hop unit is Vernon. Vernon (Chwe Hansol) was born in New York City but moved to Korea at a young age. His mother is American and his father is Korean. Vernon takes the role of the main rapper and main dancer of the group, as well as the yougest member of the unit. Through their raps, songs, and covers, they are able to display their hardships of debuting and moving up from rock bottom, as well as thanking their fans (Carats) for staying by their side.

    Performance Subunit

    The performance subunit of Seventeen consist of members Hoshi, The8, Jun, and Dino. The subunit is known for their amazing stage presence and charisma. Hoshi, the leader of the performance team, is also the main dancer, main vocalist, and rapper of Seventeen. The8 (Xu Minghao) is from China and he is the lead dancer, lead rapper and vocalist of the group. Jun (Wen Junhui) is also from China. He and The8 make up the China Line of Seventeen. Jun takes on the lead dancer, lead vocalist, and visual positions. Last but not least, Dino (Lee Chan) is the main dancer, main rapper, and vocalist of the group. He alos has the special position as the youngest member of Seventeen. Many of Seventeens choreographies are choreographed by Hoshi and the rest of the performance team. Seventeen is well known in the industry for the neatness of their choreographies, despite having so many members. This is due to their hard work and determination to present their best selves to their fans.
