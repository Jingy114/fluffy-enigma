SEVENTEEN The Group

Seventeen is a kpop group containing 13 members: S.coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, Vernon, Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, DK, Seungkwan Hoshi, Jun, The8, and Dino, that debuted on May 26, 2015 under Pledis Entertainment.

    • Vocal Subunit

    The Vocal Subunit of Seventeen consists of members Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, Dk, and Seungkwan. As the name suggests, this subunit consists of the teams main and lead vocals. They mostly sing ballads for albums and concert performances. Woozi also takes the role of the producer for the group and currently has about 60 songs copyrighted under his name.

    Hip Hop Subunit

    The Hip Hop Subunit of Seventeen consists of members S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon. This group consists most of Seventeens rappers and their music genre-you guessed it, hip hop!

    Performance Subunit

    The performance subunit of Seventeen consist of members Hoshi, The8, Jun, and Dino. The subunit is known for their amazing stage presence and charisma. Many of Seventeens choreographies are created by Hoshi and the rest of the performance team.
